Malappuram

Malappuram is situated 54 km southeast of Calicut and 90 km northwest of Palakkad.

KOLAYI VIEW POINT

Kolayi is a beautiful destination in Malappuram district of Kerala. A view point and a water fall are the main attractions of this place.

KOTTAKKUNNU

Kottakkunnu is evening hangout place. A beautifully set park situated on the top of a small hill in the middle of malappuram city.
